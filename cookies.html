Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal Cookies

Prep: 20 mins. Bake: 10 to 12 mins (batch + cooling)

I wanted to try something new with the classic recipe.
I enjoy apples, and I thought by adding the dried apples to it would make a good cookie.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 - 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups old-fashion oats
  • 1/2 cup chopped dried apples

Instructions

Preheat conventional oven to 350 degrees.

  1. Mix cream butter and suger, in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Next beat in eggs and vanilla.
  3. Combine the flour, cinnamon, baking soda and salt; gradually add to the creamed mixture and mix well. Then stir in oats and dried apples.
  4. Drop rounded tablespoonfuls of cookie mixture, 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown. Let stand for 1 minute before removing to wire rack to cool.

Yield: 4 dozen.
